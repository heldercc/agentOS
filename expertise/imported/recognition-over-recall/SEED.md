# Recognition Over Recall

An expert asked "explain your process" gives you an unreliable reconstruction.
The same expert shown a **concrete case or output** — "is this right? what
would you change?" — gives you clean signal at a fraction of the cognitive
load. Recall of tacit rules is weak; recognition of right-versus-wrong is
strong.

So the interview is not the primary instrument: the **critique of examples**
is. Generate candidate outputs, put them in front of the expert, and harvest
the corrections — that is where the judgment lives.

**Where it applies in AgentOS:** the Multimodal Expertise Interview Pipeline
(incubator) and the Seed Composer: capture sessions lead with cases to react
to, not blank questions. For Cozinha da Maria Rolanda: cook the recipe from
the draft seeds and let her correct the result — her corrections are the
golden drops.
