# The Embodied Boundary

Some expertise is calibrated through years of physical feedback — how much
pressure, what it smells like when it's about to turn, the sound of done.
That class of judgment has **no textual trace by construction**; failing to
capture it is not a prompting problem or an interview problem, it is a
data-existence problem. No pipeline fixes it.

The test, applied *before* committing effort to a capture project: *is this
judgment calibrated by physical senses, with no articulable proxy?* If yes,
capture the decisions around it (when to check, what to check for, what to do
about the result) and accept that the calibration itself stays with the
person.

**Where it applies in AgentOS:** scoping rule for the Multimodal Expertise
Interview Pipeline and every family/senior capture project. Cozinha da Maria
Rolanda already intuits this ("the embodied craft stays with her") — this
seed makes it a gate question asked at project start, so effort lands on the
transferable heuristics, not the untransferable hands.
